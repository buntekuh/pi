## Compilers

1. Niklaus Wirth — "Compiler Construction" — companion text for the Titania-0 and Titania-1 chapters. Describes a complete compiler for a simple language in under 200 pages.

## Interpreters

1. Bob Nystrom — "Crafting Interpreters" — companion text for the Titania-2 chapter. Follow Parts I and II through the Functions chapter. Titania-2 diverges from Lox at that point: no classes, no objects, no garbage collector. Available free at craftinginterpreters.com.
