[
We track general progress using the following metrics:
	
Recognition of Who I am
Recognition of what the world around me is
Recognition of sentiment towards my brothers
Recognition of sentiment towards my self
Disconnectedness from my own history
Disconnectedness of the history of the city
Disconnectedness from the real political structure of the city and who my allies really are.

The playable creature strands will be:
	
Trees are beings in total denial of who they are. They have very little choice and little opportunity for growth. At the beginning of the game, the player is a tree, watching the city around it, experiencing it as victims but also biding his time until the time, he is willing to invest in one of the other strands of being.
	
Wolfs are nefarious creatures. Wolfs feed on the weak including and especially the trees. Wolf are natural predators, they control illegal goods, enjoy the good life but ultimately will pay the price of being eliminated by rivals.

Fae are the common people of the city of lights. They are those, who have carved a niche for themselves, work as traders or artisans or factory workers and keep the city running without being able to achieve greatness.

Crows are those who live a life of denial. A life of seeming freedom from responsibility. However they are unable to cope with tragic events, of which believe me there will be many in our city life. They are the hippies who can only survive if the rest of the city gives them the freedom to be who they are.

The stork, is a person who takes responsibilty for themselves and others, Storks have the freedom to leave the city, to find the truth about their past and come to terms with who they are.

Finally there is wo/man. Wo/man is a strand that begins where the stork leaves off. The being of wo/man is accepted through the recognition of inner wisdom and the acceptance of the own path.

There are many and varied dances, too numerous to mention and their merit is as individual as their respective name and nature.

Open Space in front of Station is a room. "It is an open space for trees to root. For arrivals to rest. Yet also there is laughter of fey folk and birds."
It is east of the Train station.

Market is a room. "It is a fey market. fruit, vegetables, shawls and toys. A bustle of people, delighted exclamation at the sight of a cute sash or when haggling outrageous prices."
It is south of the Open Space in front of Station.

Sprawling Forest of Trees is a room. "It is a sprawling jumble of trees, young saplings and old gnarled oaks stretch away into the distance."
It is south of the Market.

Chapter 2 - Arrival

Test Arrival with "z / z / z"

When play begins:
	say "Uprooted from my ancestral home, I finally arrive in the City of Lights.[line break]I have forgotten the dances of my homeland.[line break]I have forgotten who I am.[paragraph break]All I want, is to take root."

Arrival is a Scene. 

Arrival begins when the player is in the Train station for the first turn.
Arrival ends when the player is in the Sprawling Forest three turns.

every turn during the Arrival:
	if player is in the Open Space in front of Station:
		say "A jumble of trees, trembling, a forlorn rustle, lost.[line break]";
	if player is in the Market:
		say "Startled frightened fey call out as a stampede of scuffling trees bears down on the market. A frenzy of flurry as merchants hustle to push their goods out of the way.";
	if player is in the Sprawling Forest of Trees:
		say "Trees are spreading out, taking root.[line break]The stampede dissipates into a sudden calm serenity.[line break]";
		say "My roots tentatively sprout deeper into the earth.";
		say "A sigh of relief. [line break]";
		say "A home? I cannot say. Yet at least a place to rest."

automatic going is initially false.

check going during the Arrival:
	if automatic going is false:
		say "I feel too listless, tired and lost to do anything." instead.

Every turn during the Arrival:	
	if the location of the player is not the Sprawling Forest of Trees:
		if Arrival is happening:	
			say "Shuffling feet push from behind. A surge of mighty trees trundling along sweep me away.";
		otherwise:
			say "Anxiously I head for a forest to rest.";
		let the way be the best route from the location of the player to the Sprawling Forest of Trees;
		now automatic going is true;
		try the player going the way;
		now automatic going is false.
